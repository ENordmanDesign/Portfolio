Social Media Branding

Branding Design | Social Media Marketing

Project Scope

Objective

Use established logos and expand a brand with colors and fonts to be used across social media and email marketing.

Deliverables

A redesign of the brands instagram home page with new posts, and a graphic standard for the brand.

  • Timeline

    • 1 Month

  • My Roles

    • Designer, Photographer

  • Tools

    • Adobe Illustrator, Instagram, Link Tree

  • Constraints

    • I couldn’t change any of the established logos that the company had been using for 40+ years so I was only allowed to build off of those logos and some colors they used in their wine labels.

Research

1 I wanted to see what was working for other companies on Instagram but also see what recent designs others were using that weren't being used by a company yet. There are a lot of great designs out there on Dribble and Pinterest but it’s good to see what is actually working.

2 One of the biggest obstacles with this company was that they had so many logos. They had two logos for each of their tasting rooms, one logo for the wine company itself, and then a third logo for their clubs and high end line. I needed fonts and colors that could work with all of them.

3 I also wanted a baseline for the instagram posts so that they all had similar formatting. This would also speed up the rate at which we could make a post. By creating a general template, it would create design consistency through the online brand and optimize the process.
